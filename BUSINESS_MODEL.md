# 💼 ZingBot Cloud Business Model

## Overview

ZingBot Cloud is a Software as a Service (SaaS) platform designed to simplify Web3 community automation.

Our business model focuses on recurring subscriptions and value-added services.

---

## Subscription Plans

### 🆓 Starter
- Free
- Basic automation
- Limited bots

### ⭐ Pro
- $3/month
- Advanced automation
- More bots
- Analytics

### 🏢 Business
- $10/month
- Unlimited workspaces
- Team management
- Premium support

---

## Revenue Streams

- Monthly subscriptions
- Annual subscriptions
- Premium features
- Enterprise solutions
- Future Marketplace

---

## Affiliate Program

Users can earn commissions by referring new customers to ZingBot Cloud.

## Long-Term Vision

Our objective is to build a sustainable platform serving thousands of Web3 creators and businesses worldwide.
